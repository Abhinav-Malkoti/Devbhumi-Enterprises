Our Services

Comprehensive Solutions for Railway Infrastructure

Overhead Equipment (OHE)

We specialize in the supply, erection, testing, and commissioning of 25kV AC OHE systems. Our capabilities include:

  • Foundation casting and mast erection.
  • Wiring (Catenary and Contact wire) and droppering.
  • Bracket assembly and adjustment.
  • Tower Wagon checking and final commissioning.

Traction Substations (TSS)

Expert handling of high-voltage traction substations critical for railway power supply.

  • Installation of Power Transformers (132kV/25kV).
  • Circuit Breakers, Isolators, and Interrupters.
  • Control and Relay panel wiring.
  • SCADA interfacing and testing.

Consultancy & Survey

End-to-end consultancy for railway electrification projects.

  • Location survey and pegging.
  • Profile drawing and L-section preparation.
  • Quantity estimation and project planning.
